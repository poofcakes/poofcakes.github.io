Word Scramble

Unscramble gaming words before the 45-second timer runs out.

Mini Games / Word Scramble

How to Play

Press Start Game and read the scrambled letters on screen. Type the correct word in the answer box. When your guess matches the full word, it counts automatically and the next puzzle appears.

Words are gaming-themed: class roles, MMO slang, and titles from games Poofcakes covers. You have 45 seconds to solve as many as possible. Your best total is saved in this browser.

Tips

  • Look for common suffixes like -ING or doubled letters that stand out.
  • Short words like LOOT and BOSS are free points if you spot them fast.
  • Press Enter to submit if auto-check does not trigger on your device.
