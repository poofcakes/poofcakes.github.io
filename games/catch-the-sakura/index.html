Catch the Sakura

A cozy 20-second clicker. Grab as many falling petals as you can.

Mini Games / Catch the Sakura

How to Play

Press Start Game and click sakura petals as they drift down the play area. Each click counts as one point. Petals spawn continuously until the 20-second timer hits zero.

If a petal reaches the bottom without being clicked, it simply disappears. There is no penalty, but you do lose a scoring chance. Your best score is saved locally in this browser.

Tips

  • Scan the full width of the field instead of locking onto one spot.
  • Click early on fast-falling petals near the top.
  • This game also appears on the homepage for a quicker round.
