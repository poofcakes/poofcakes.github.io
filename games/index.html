Poofcakes Mini Games

Tiny browser games for quick breaks between grinding dailies or reading patch notes. Each game has its own page with rules, tips, and a personal best tracker stored in your browser.

Pick a Game

All games run in your browser with no install and no account. Rounds are short (20 to 45 seconds), so they work well during queue times or loading screens. High scores stay on your device until you clear site data.

Typing · 30 sec

Key Pop

Type falling keycap letters before they fade. Build combos for bonus points in this fast typing sprint.

Play Key Pop

Clicker · 20 sec

Catch the Sakura

Click drifting sakura petals as they fall. A cozy reflex game that also lives on the homepage.

Play Catch the Sakura

Reflex · 5 rounds

Reaction Tap

Wait for green, then click. Measures your reaction time across five rounds with a running average.

Play Reaction Tap

Word Scramble

Unscramble gaming-themed words as fast as you can. Answers check automatically when you finish typing.
