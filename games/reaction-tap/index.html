Reaction Tap

Wait for green, then click. A simple five-round reflex benchmark.

Mini Games / Reaction Tap

How to Play

Hit Start Game. The pad turns red and says "Wait for green." Do not click yet. After a random delay, the pad turns green. Click immediately to record your reaction time in milliseconds.

You play five rounds per session. Your average across those five rounds is compared against your stored personal best. Click too early on a red pad and you get a false start, ending that attempt.

What Is a Good Score?

  • Under 250 ms is sharp for most players on a desktop setup.
  • 250 to 350 ms is very common and perfectly fine.
  • Mobile touch screens often run a little slower than mouse clicks.
