Key Pop

A 30-second typing sprint. Pop keycaps before they fade and chase a higher combo.

Mini Games / Key Pop

How to Play

Click Start Game, then type the letter shown on each falling keycap. Every correct key earns a point. Miss a cap or type the wrong letter and your combo resets.

Every fifth combo in a row gives a small bonus. At the end of 30 seconds you will see your total pops and an estimated keys-per-minute score. Your best run is saved in this browser automatically.

Tips

  • Keep your eyes on the newest caps near the top of the play area.
  • Prioritize caps that are about to fade rather than clearing old ones first.
  • A steady rhythm beats frantic mashing once combos start stacking.
