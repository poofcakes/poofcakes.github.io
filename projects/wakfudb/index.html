WakfuDB

A searchable encyclopedia for Wakfu players.

Launch WakfuDB

What It Does

Wakfu has deep systems - gear, resources, monsters, recipes, and build planning all spread across wikis and forum posts. WakfuDB brings the reference data you need into one fast, searchable database designed for everyday play.

Key Features

  • Item & equipment database - look up stats, requirements, and sources
  • Monster references - find drops, locations, and related info
  • Resource & crafting data - plan gathering and production routes
  • Fast search - get to the right page in seconds
  • Mobile-friendly layout - browse on a second monitor or your phone

Who It's For

New players learning what gear to chase, veterans theorycrafting builds, and anyone tired of tab-hopping between outdated wiki pages. WakfuDB is built to be the tab you keep open while playing.

How to Use It

  1. Open the database - click Launch WakfuDB above. It loads in your browser with no sign-in.
  2. Search for what you need - type an item, monster, or resource name into the search bar. Results filter as you type, so partial names work.
  3. Open an entry - click a result to see its full details: stats, level requirements, drop sources, and related items.
  4. Compare options - keep WakfuDB open on a second monitor while you play and jump between entries to compare gear for a build.
  5. Plan crafting and gathering - look up recipes and resource entries to map out what to gather before a crafting session.

Because everything runs in the browser, you can bookmark a specific entry and come back to it later, or share a link with a guildmate who's chasing the same drop.

FAQ

Is WakfuDB official?

No. WakfuDB is a fan project and is not affiliated with Ankama. Game content belongs to its respective owners.

How often is data updated?

Updates follow game patches and community discoveries. Major patches may take a short time to reflect fully in the database.

Can I suggest missing entries?

Yes - reach out via the contact page with the item or monster name and any details you have.
