Pixels

Fuse bead patterns from pixel art, in your browser.

Launch Pixels

What It Does

Fuse beads (Perler-style crafts) need clear, color-accurate patterns. Pixels takes pixel art - whether from a game sprite, original artwork, or a small illustration - and generates a grid layout you can follow bead by bead.

Key Features

  • Pattern generation - convert pixel grids into bead layouts
  • Craft-friendly output - designed for real-world bead projects
  • Browser workflow - no desktop software required
  • Pixel art planning - preview before you melt a single bead

Who It's For

Crafters, pixel artists, and gamers who want physical merch from their favorite sprites. Great for keychains, coasters, wall art, and gifts.

How to Use It

  1. Open Pixels - click Launch Pixels above. No account or install needed.
  2. Add your pixel art - bring in a sprite, original drawing, or small illustration to use as the base for your pattern.
  3. Generate the grid - Pixels maps your image onto a bead grid, with each cell representing one bead.
  4. Review the palette - check the generated color list against the bead colors you actually own before you start.
  5. Follow it bead by bead - use the grid as a reference while you place beads on your pegboard, then fuse when you're done.

Tips for Best Results

  • Start with simple sprites with limited colors
  • Keep designs small enough to finish in one sitting
  • Check your bead color inventory against the generated palette before starting
  • Use a clear pegboard and good lighting when placing beads

FAQ

Do I need an account?

No - open the app and start creating.

Can I use game sprites?

You can use sprites for personal craft projects, but respect copyright and trademark rules for anything you sell or distribute commercially.
