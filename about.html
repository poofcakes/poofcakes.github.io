About Poofcakes

Developer, streamer, and tool-maker for the games I play every day.

Who I Am

I'm Poofcakes - a solo developer and content creator who builds free web tools for gaming communities. I stream on Twitch, share videos on YouTube, and spend a lot of time turning frustrating in-game spreadsheet work into something anyone can use in a browser.

What This Site Is

Poofcakes.com is the home base for my projects and written content. You'll find launch pages for my apps, blog posts for the games I cover, and dev notes explaining how the tools work. Everything here is built and maintained by me.

Projects I Maintain

  • BP:SR Event Tracker - event schedules for Blue Protocol: Star Resonance
  • WakfuDB - a Wakfu item and monster encyclopedia
  • wzbrowser - in-browser MapleStory WZ file explorer
  • Pixels - fuse bead pattern generator for pixel art crafts

Why I Build These Tools

Game wikis and spreadsheets are often outdated, hard to search, or spread across dozens of tabs. I build focused tools that solve one problem well: track events on a timeline, look up an item in seconds, preview game assets locally, or plan a craft project from pixel art.

All of my browser tools run client-side where possible - your files stay on your machine, and there's no account required to get started.
